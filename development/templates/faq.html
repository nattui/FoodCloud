Frequently Asked Questions

1. What does FoodCloud do?SIEBEL

FoodCloud's mission is reducing food waste and encourging businesses and customers to partake in sustainable lifestyles. On our plateform, businesses post food sales before their close time to reduce their food waste for the day, and customers receive information about those cheaper food options based on their locations and how far they are willing to travel. The platform benefits both comsumers and providers, and aims to ameliorate the issue of food water in the United States.

2. How do I get involved as a business?

Business can sign up on our signup page with an email and zipcode. After logging into your account, you are redirected to a webpage where pictures, descriptions and prices of the food can be uploaded before the business closing time.

3. How do I get involved as a customer?

Customers can search for food sales around a N mile radius from a zipcode. If there are businesses from those zipcode area, the food information will be on the customer's feed.

4. Are there any fees or costs to join?

The service is free to join for businesses and free to use for customers :)
